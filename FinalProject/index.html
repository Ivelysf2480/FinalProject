What's Happening

Animals are humanely killed (usually by lethal injection) when they have been there beyond a certain time or the shelter has physically run out of space. There are also “no-kill” shelters, but these can end up turning many dogs away to far worse fates since they end up full of unadoptable animals.

Adopt, don’t shop – shelter pets have every bit as much love to give as one from a breeder. If you do none of these things, then do this at least look closely at those shelter workers and think about the pain they willingly take on every day because someone else chose not to hold up their end of the bargain.
